Ecogalapagos

Our mission helps us to accomplish three main functions through our non-profit establishment: to provide a hands on study abroad program for youth across the world; to create a youth center in San Cristobal, Galapagos designed to enrich the lives of students with educational, cultural, and recreational activities; and to establish a world class volunteer experience for students, teachers, and others.

Who We Are

We are EcoGalapagos. We aim to improve education through both method - effective project-based learning - and material - by teaching skills that are applicable to improving your life today.
